**Short description** (≤80 chars)

```

**Full description** (≤4000 chars)
```
SiteOS is an all-in-one construction project management app for builders, contractors, and site teams. It keeps every project, site, and team member organized — from labour attendance to material stock, machinery, bills, and on-site documentation.

SiteOS is an invite-only workplace tool. Accounts are created by your organization's administrator with role-based access for admins and site managers, so everyone sees exactly what they need.

KEY FEATURES

• Projects & Sites — Track every construction project and its sites in one dashboard with real-time updates.
• Labour Management — Record workers, attendance, and assignments across sites.
• Stock & Materials — Monitor material inventory, receipts, and usage to avoid shortages.
• Machinery — Keep tabs on equipment assigned to each site.
• Bills & Expenses — Create, review, and export bills with PDF generation.
• Blueprints & Documents — Upload, view, and share project blueprints and PDFs.
• Site Photos — Capture site progress, material receipts, and documents directly with your camera.
• Reports — Generate and export project reports as PDFs.
• Role-Based Access — Super admins, admins, and site managers each get the right permissions.
• Works on the move — Offline-friendly caching keeps your data available with intermittent connectivity.

Built for construction firms that want to replace scattered spreadsheets and paperwork with a single, secure, real-time system.

Your data is protected with row-level security and encrypted connections. See our privacy policy at https://clivi-privacy.web.app

Note: SiteOS requires an account provided by your organization. There is no public sign-up.
```
