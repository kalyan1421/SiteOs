Construction projects, labour, stock & site photos in one app.

Need help?

Civil Pro is an invite-only app for construction teams. If you have a question, a problem, or a data request, reach out and we'll get back to you.

Email Support Privacy Policy

Typical response time: 1–2 business days.

Getting started

Accounts are created by your organization's administrator — there is no public sign-up. If you don't have login details, contact your project admin or super admin.

Frequently asked

I can't log in / forgot my password

Use the "Forgot password" option on the sign-in screen, or ask your administrator to reset your account. Accounts are managed by your organization's admin.

How do I add site photos?

Open a project's site, tap the photo action, and either capture a new photo with your camera or pick one from your library. Civil Pro needs camera and photo permissions for this.

Why does the app ask for camera and photo access?

Only to capture and upload site photos, material receipts, and documents. Permissions are requested when you use those features and can be revoked anytime in device settings.

Does it work offline?

Yes — recently loaded data is cached so you can keep working with intermittent connectivity. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I delete my account or data?

Email support@clivimanagement.app or ask your administrator. See our Privacy Policy for details.
