Privacy Policy — SiteOS

Last updated: 7 June 2026

SiteOS ("the App", "we", "us") is a construction project management application for authorized staff of the operating organization. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Who can use the App

The App is restricted to invited users (super admins, admins, and site managers). Accounts are created by an administrator; there is no public sign-up.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email address, role, and the projects/sites assigned to you. Used for authentication and access control.
  • Operational data you enter: project, labour, stock, machinery, bills, blueprints, and report records.
  • Photos & files: images captured with your camera or chosen from your library, and documents you upload (e.g. material receipts, site photos, PDFs).
  • Device & diagnostic data: basic network connectivity status used to support offline syncing.

3. Device permissions

  • Camera — to capture site photos, receipts, and documents.
  • Photos / storage — to select and upload existing images and files.

Permissions are requested only when you use the related feature, and you may decline or revoke them in your device settings.

4. How we use your data

Data is used solely to operate the App: authenticating you, displaying and syncing the projects and records you are authorized to access, and storing the photos and files you submit. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

5. Where data is stored

Data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database, file storage, and authentication) with row-level security so users can only access records permitted by their role. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

6. Data sharing

We share data only with our infrastructure provider (Supabase) strictly to deliver the service. We do not share data with third parties for marketing.

7. Data retention & deletion

Records are retained while your organization uses the App. To request deletion of your account or associated data, contact us at the address below; an administrator can also deactivate accounts and remove records.

8. Children

The App is a workplace tool and is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.

10. Contact

Questions or data requests: support@siteos.in
